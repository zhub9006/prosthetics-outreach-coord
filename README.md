# Prosthetic Outreach Initiative

Coordination hub for volunteer tasks across our prosthetic outreach teams in **Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta**.

## Goals
- Identify and promote affordable prosthetic solutions
- Coordinate volunteer efforts across regions
- Track clinical trials and research partnerships
- Plan outreach events and community engagement

## Team locations
- 📍 **Chicago, IL**
- 📍 **Boston, MA**  
- 📍 **Atlanta, GA**

## How to use
- Create issues for tasks and volunteer assignments
- Use [Project boards](https://github.com/users/zhub9006/projects) to track progress
- Label issues by region (Chicago/Boston/Atlanta) and type (research/voltage/logistics)
